Careers in water and wastewater plant operation offer great pay and benefits, room for advancement, and a stable, recession-proof job market. Our operator training courses can help you start a new career or enhance your existing one.

Stormwater Certification

OWP provides CASQA with administrative and technical support for its training programs for the Construction and Industrial General Permits. This online service enables qualified professionals to set up an account where they can take exams, access CGP and IGP publications, configure contact information for the free Public Lookup tool, and complete QISP training.

Research


Our research group engineers and scientists perform and manage research efforts focused on characterizing water quality issues, including developing and testing methods to improve water quality. Our researchers provide:

  • Technical advice on water policy issues
  • Assistance in watershed planning
  • Cost assessments and data analysis and modeling
  • Help in making informed decisions based on accurate information and good science
