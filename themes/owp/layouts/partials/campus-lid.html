Campus LID Project

The Campus LID Project brought together a diverse group of organizations to create significant environmental and social improvements through designing and installing a variety of LID BMPs that fit into existing infrastructure and creating ongoing educational opportunities for the public, university students, stormwater professionals, and construction contractors.

OWP is the 2015 Urban Development Project Award Winner!

The American Society of Civil Engineers (ASCE) Sacramento Section awarded the 2015 Urban Development Project of the Year to the Campus LID Project! We are very proud of our team that worked to make this project such a success.
