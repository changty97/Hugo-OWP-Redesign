Research


Our research group engineers and scientists perform and manage research efforts focused on characterizing water quality issues, including developing and testing methods to improve water quality. Our researchers provide:

  • Technical advice on water policy issues
  • Assistance in watershed planning
  • Cost assessments and data analysis and modeling
  • Help in making informed decisions based on accurate information and good science
